## 📄 论文阅读清单

### Tier 1：必读论文

#### 自动驾驶端到端
1. **UniAD: Planning-oriented Autonomous Driving** (CVPR 2023 Best Paper)
   - [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.10156)
   - [GitHub](https://github.com/OpenDriveLab/UniAD)
   - [项目主页](https://opendrivelab.github.io/UniAD/)
   - **重点**：整体架构设计，模块间交互

2. **VAD: Vectorized Autonomous Driving** (ICCV 2023)
   - [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2303.12077)
   - [GitHub](https://github.com/hustvl/VAD)
   - **重点**：向量化表示，效率优化

3. **BEVFormer: Learning Bird's-Eye-View Representation** (ECCV 2022)
   - [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2203.17270)
   - [GitHub](https://github.com/fundamentalvision/BEVFormer)
   - **重点**：BEV 感知，Transformer

#### 自动对焦
4. **Deep Learning for Camera Autofocus** (IEEE TCI 2021)
   - [IEEE Xplore](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9356970)
   - [GitHub](https://github.com/ChengyuWang1007/Deep-Learning-for-Camera-Autofocus)
   - **重点**：CNN 焦点估计，端到端对焦

5. **Deep Reinforcement Learning-Based Camera Autofocus** 
   - [IEEE Xplore](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9356970)
   - **重点**：强化学习对焦控制

6. **Autofocus control using adaptive region selection and RL** (Optics Express 2025)
   - [Optica](https://opg.optica.org/oe/fulltext.cfm?uri=oe-33-8-17345)
   - **重点**：自适应区域选择，个性化对焦
